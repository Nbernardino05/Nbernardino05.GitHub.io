Welcome to the Green Neighbor Project

We are a community-driven, volunteer-based endeavor that offers free lawn care services to the elderly, disabled, or to those just in need of a helping hand in Wilmington, NC.

Whether you are a resident in need of lawn care services or a volunteer looking to give back to the community, we invite you to explore our website and learn more about our mission and how you can get involved.
