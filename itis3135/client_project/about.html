About Us

The Green Neighbor Project is a community-driven, volunteer-based initiative that provides free lawn care services to the elderly, disabled, or those in need of assistance in Wilmington, NC.

Our mission is to provide assistance to those who may not be able to maintain their lawns due to physical limitations or other challenges. We believe that everyone deserves a well-kept yard, and we are here to help make that happen.

Thank you for visiting the Green Neighbor Project. We look forward to working with you to make our community a better place!
