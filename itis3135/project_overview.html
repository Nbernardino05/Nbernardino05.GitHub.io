Client Project Overview

Project Overview:

Purpose: The Green Neighbor Project is a community-driven, volunteer-based endeavor that offers free lawn care services to the elderly, disabled, or to those just in need of a helping hand in Wilmington, NC. This website will serve as an informational hub for volunteers and community members alike. It will highlight the mission of the organization, provide a way for residents to request services, and allow interested individuals to sign up as volunteers.

Intended Users:

  • Community members in need of lawn care assistance
  • Volunteers looking for service opportunities

Content Summary:

  • About us section with mission and values
  • Lawn service request form
  • Volunteer registration form
  • Contact page
  • Gallery of before/after service photos
