Client Project Overview

Project Overview:

Purpose:

The Green Neighbor Project is a community-driven, volunteer-based endeavor that offers free lawn care services to the elderly, disabled, or to those just in need of a helping hand in Wilmington, NC. This website will serve as an informational hub for volunteers and community members alike. It will highlight the mission of the organization, provide a way for residents to request services, and allow interested individuals to sign up as volunteers.

Intended Users:

  • Community members in need of lawn care assistance
  • Volunteers looking for service opportunities

Content Summary:

  • About us section with mission and values
  • Lawn service request form
  • Volunteer registration form
  • FAQ page
  • Slideshow of finished service projects

Wireframe:

Page Design:

Home/Landing (page 1)

  • Purpose: Welcome visitors, introduce the community project, and then direct them to request service page.
  • Users: General public, potential volunteers, neighbors who need help.
  • Content: Project description, call to action, and photo slideshow of previous lawn projects.
  • Data Entry: none
  • Data Validation: none
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: none
  • Actions: Users read through content.

Request Service (page 2)

  • Purpose: Allow community members to register their household for volunteer lawn care assistance.
  • Users: Potential clients
  • Content: Brief explanation of who can sign up, how the program works, and an easy request form.
  • Data Entry: Request form (name, address, phone number, email, service needed selection).
  • Data Validation: Required fields (name, address, email/phone), validate email/phone format.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Submit button
  • Actions: Users fill out and submit the registration/request form.

Get Involved (page 3)

  • Purpose: Encourage users to volunteer.
  • Users: Potential volunteers.
  • Content: Volunteer role description and sign-up form.
  • Data Entry: Sign-up form (name, phone number, email, availibility slection, additional information text box).
  • Data Validation: Required fields (name, email/phone, address, availibility), validate email/phone format.
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Submit button
  • Actions: Users fill out and submit the volunteer sign-up form.

About Us (page 4)

  • Purpose: Mission statement, origin story, and history of the project.
  • Users: Potential volunteers and clients.
  • Content: Mission statement, origin story, and favorite works photo slideshow.
  • Data Entry: none
  • Data Validation: none
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Two buttons to move to next image or return to previous picture in gallery.
  • Actions: Users read content and click through photo slideshow.

FAQ (page 5)

  • Purpose: Answer common questions about the volunteer lawn care project, eligibility, and how to get involved.
  • Users: Prospective clients.
  • Content: List of frequently asked questions and answers (example: “Who qualifies for help?”, “How can I volunteer?”).
  • Data Entry: none
  • Data Validation: none
  • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Buttons to open FAQ answers.
  • Actions: Users search for their question, if they have one.

Dynamic Functionality:

Description & Purpose:

  • Dynamic Photo Slideshow (on About page): Allow users to browse project photos with a clickable slideshow or carousel, making the page more visually engaging
  • Form Validation (on Get Involved and Request Lawn Care Assistance pages): Ensure that users complete all required fields (such as name, email, address) correctly before submitting forms, improving data quality and preventing errors.
  • Accordion (on FAQ page): Enable users to expand or collapse answers to frequently asked questions by clicking, saving space and improving readability.
