Website Evaluations

Good Design: Duolingo

Duolingo is a language-learning platform that offers interactive lessons. This website is the company's home page and it features the benefits that the learning service provides.

  • Contrast: The bright green and blue color scheme creates clear visual distinctions. The white space and bold text also make buttons and sections easy to read.
  • Repetition: The design is consistent across pages, with similar fonts, buttons, and layouts. Icons and illustrations also maintain a cohesive cartoonish style throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Text, buttons, and images are placed logically, guiding the user's eye smoothly. This page maintains visual balance by alternating the placement of content on both sides, creating a structured and engaging layout.
  • Proximity: Related elements (like like features and their related image) are grouped together. White space is used effectively to separate different sections, preventing clutter. Navigation menus are close to relevant content, making it intuitive.
